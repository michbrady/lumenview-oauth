LumenView – Data Deletion Instructions

LumenView uses Facebook Login solely to access your photos within the app on your Meta Quest headset. We do not store your data on any server.

The only data stored is an OAuth access token saved locally on your headset in Unity PlayerPrefs. It is never transmitted to any third party.

To delete your data

  1. Open LumenView on your Meta Quest.
  2. In the menu, tap Log Out under the Meta Gallery section. This immediately deletes the locally stored access token.

Alternatively, uninstalling LumenView from your headset removes all locally stored data.

To revoke LumenView's access to your Facebook account entirely, visit Facebook App Settings and remove LumenView from the list of connected apps.
